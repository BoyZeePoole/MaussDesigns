About

Hello! My name is Jeanine and I am the creator of Mauss.

A quick overview of my background – I have a Bachelor’s degree in Visual Communication and I am also a full time Art Director.

I have always had a deep fascination and love for design, whether it be architecture, furniture, fashion or graphic design.

The Mauss story began 3 years ago with the birth of my baby niece. I wanted to create a special and unique heirloom piece for her, free of synthetics and mass produced themes.
Inspired by my mother, her creativity and incredible talent with needle and thread which she passed onto me, my first mobile was born!

I continued further, and what started as a labour of love for my niece soon grew into a tradition that saw friends, family and friends of friends reaching out for their own individual mobile, each telling a unique story with colourful and imaginative characters.

Mauss is the embodiment of imagination, celebration of individuality and love, sharing it one mobile at a time. Thank you for joining us on our journey!
